# BHOS Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 10, 2026**

## Introduction

BHOS ("Behavioral Home Operating System", "we", "us", "our") operates the BHOS mobile application. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Name, email address, and role within your organization
- Authentication credentials managed securely through our authentication provider (Clerk)

### Clinical Data
- Patient records, medication administration logs, and treatment documentation
- Incident reports and daily care logs
- Vital signs and clinical assessments
- This data is entered by authorized staff and belongs to your organization

### Location Data
- GPS coordinates during shift clock-in/out to verify staff presence at authorized group home locations
- Location data is only collected during active clock-in events and is not continuously tracked

### Camera & Barcode Data
- Camera access is used exclusively for scanning medication barcodes (NDC, UPC, EAN, QR, Code128, DataMatrix formats) during medication administration
- Scanned barcode data is matched against your organization's medication records to verify the correct medication
- Barcode verification results are recorded in the medication administration log for audit compliance
- Camera images are processed on-device for barcode detection and are not stored or transmitted

### Device Information
- Device identifiers for device enrollment and security verification
- Biometric authentication capability (Face ID/Touch ID) — biometric data never leaves your device
- Push notification tokens for receiving alerts

### Usage Data
- App interaction logs for security audit trails (HIPAA requirement)
- Error reports for application stability

## How We Use Your Information

- **Service Delivery**: To provide medication management, shift tracking, patient care documentation, and compliance features
- **Safety & Compliance**: To maintain audit trails required by HIPAA and state behavioral health regulations
- **Security**: To verify authorized access through biometric authentication, PIN verification, and device enrollment
- **Location Verification**: To confirm staff are physically present at group home locations during shift operations
- **Notifications**: To send critical alerts about medication schedules, incidents, and access requests

## Data Security

- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest
- HIPAA-compliant data handling and storage practices
- Role-based access controls ensure staff only see data relevant to their responsibilities
- Complete audit trail of all data access and modifications
- Biometric data is processed entirely on-device and never transmitted to our servers

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We share data only:
- Within your organization as determined by your organization's administrators
- With our service providers who assist in operating the platform (hosting, authentication)
- When required by law or to comply with legal obligations

## Multi-Tenant Data Isolation

Each organization's data is completely isolated. Staff at one organization can never access another organization's patients, medications, or records.

## Data Retention

- Clinical records are retained according to your organization's policies and applicable state regulations
- Account data is retained while your account is active
- Audit logs are retained for the minimum period required by HIPAA (6 years)
- You may request data export or deletion by contacting your organization's administrator

## Your Rights

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Export**: Request a machine-readable export of your data

## Children's Privacy

BHOS is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
