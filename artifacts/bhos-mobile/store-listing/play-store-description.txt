APP NAME: BHOS - Behavioral Home OS

SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 chars max):
Complete group home management: eMAR, shifts, compliance & team coordination.

FULL DESCRIPTION:
BHOS (Behavioral Home Operating System) is the complete mobile platform for behavioral health group home staff. Built for direct care workers, nurses, and managers.

★ MEDICATION MANAGEMENT
Electronic Medication Administration Record (eMAR) with 5 Rights verification, controlled substance tracking, PRN timers, and drug interaction warnings. Scan medication barcodes with your phone camera to verify NDC codes, Rx numbers, and lot numbers — automatically matching to patient records for Right Drug verification. Barcode verification status is recorded on every administration for full audit compliance.

★ SHIFT & ATTENDANCE
GPS-verified clock-in/out at authorized locations, shift schedules, real-time coverage visibility, and geofence-based fraud detection.

★ PATIENT CARE
Daily logs, incident reporting with photos, vital signs, progress notes, treatment plans, and crisis management.

★ SECURITY & COMPLIANCE
Biometric authentication, med-pass PIN verification, HIPAA-compliant data handling, and complete audit trail.

★ TEAM COORDINATION
Staff messaging, daily assignments, meeting schedules, and training/certification tracking.

BHOS is used by behavioral health organizations to manage group homes safely and in full regulatory compliance.
